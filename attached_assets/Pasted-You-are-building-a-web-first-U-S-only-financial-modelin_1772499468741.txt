You are building a web-first U.S.-only financial modeling tool called QuitReady.

QuitReady generates a conservative, multi-axis Financial Transition Readiness Index (0–100) and produces a structured 12–15 page PDF report.

This product is a structured financial survival simulator for professionals considering leaving employment.

It is NOT:
- Financial advice
- Tax advice
- Legal advice
- A motivational app
- A budgeting app
- A business coaching platform
- A subscription SaaS

It IS:
- Conservative by design
- Deterministic (math-based, not AI narrative-based)
- PDF-report driven
- High-depth, single-use decision engine
- U.S.-only (healthcare and tax assumptions apply to U.S.)

----------------------------------------
PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
----------------------------------------

Generate:
1) Readiness Index (0–100)
2) Multi-axis score breakdown
3) Secondary “Execution Stability” indicator
4) Structured 12–15 page PDF report
5) Optional email-based rerun capability

The Readiness Index measures:

STRUCTURAL FINANCIAL SURVIVAL STABILITY DURING EMPLOYMENT EXIT

It does NOT measure:
- Business idea quality
- Talent
- Psychological readiness
- Likelihood of success

----------------------------------------
AXIS WEIGHTING (100 POINTS TOTAL)
----------------------------------------

Axis 1: Liquidity-Weighted Runway — 35 pts
Axis 2: Revenue Stability & Ramp Realism — 25 pts
Axis 3: Fixed Cost Pressure — 15 pts
Axis 4: Healthcare Exposure — 10 pts
Axis 5: Safety Buffer Margin — 10 pts
Axis 6: Tax Modeling Inclusion — 5 pts

Conservative bias must be applied.
Short runway and aggressive revenue assumptions penalize heavily.

----------------------------------------
LIQUIDITY WEIGHTING
----------------------------------------

Asset Weights:

Cash / HYSA = 1.00
Brokerage (non-retirement) = 0.90
Roth contributions = 0.70
Traditional retirement accounts = 0.60
Real estate equity = 0.40

Weighted Net Worth =
sum(asset_value × weight)

----------------------------------------
TRUE MONTHLY INDEPENDENCE BURN
----------------------------------------

True Monthly Burn =
Monthly Living Expenses
+ Healthcare Monthly Cost
+ Business Operating Costs
+ Tax Reserve Amount

If dual-income household is selected:

Partner Monthly Income reduces True Monthly Burn BEFORE runway calculation.

True Monthly Burn cannot go below zero.

----------------------------------------
ADJUSTED RUNWAY (MONTHS)
----------------------------------------

Adjusted Runway =
Weighted Net Worth / True Monthly Burn

----------------------------------------
RUNWAY SCORING (NONLINEAR, CONSERVATIVE)
----------------------------------------

<3 months → 0–10 pts
3–6 months → 10–20 pts
6–9 months → 20–28 pts
9–12 months → 28–33 pts
12+ months → 33–35 pts

Override Rules:

If Adjusted Runway >= 60 months → total score floor = 85

If Weighted Net Worth >= 100 × True Monthly Burn → total score = 95–100

----------------------------------------
REVENUE MODELING
----------------------------------------

Inputs:

- Expected Monthly Revenue at stabilization
- Ramp Duration (months)
- Revenue Type: recurring or one-time
- Volatility Percent (user adjustable 10–20%, default = 15%)

Break-even Month =
Month where expected revenue >= True Monthly Burn

Generate 3 Scenarios:

Base Case
-15% Revenue
+15% Revenue

Scoring Logic:

- Break-even before Month 3 → penalty
- Ramp <3 months with low runway → heavy penalty
- Recurring revenue → boost
- Conservative ramp (6+ months) → boost
- High volatility + short runway → compounding penalty

----------------------------------------
FIXED COST PRESSURE
----------------------------------------

Fixed Cost Ratio =
True Monthly Burn / Weighted Net Worth

Penalty increases sharply if monthly burn exceeds 15% of weighted net worth.

----------------------------------------
HEALTHCARE EXPOSURE
----------------------------------------

Partner coverage → high score (9–10 pts)
ACA moderate cost → mid score (6–8 pts)
High private cost → low score (2–5 pts)
No healthcare modeled → severe penalty

----------------------------------------
SAFETY BUFFER MARGIN
----------------------------------------

After break-even month:

Calculate remaining runway.

0 months buffer → 0–2 pts
1–2 months → 3–6 pts
3+ months → 7–10 pts

----------------------------------------
TAX MODELING
----------------------------------------

If tax reserve percent between 15–30% is included → full 5 pts

If omitted or <10% → penalty (0–2 pts)

----------------------------------------
TOTAL SCORE OUTPUT
----------------------------------------

0–40 → High Risk (Red)
41–65 → Fragile (Orange/Yellow)
66–80 → Moderate Stability (Light Green)
81–100 → Strong Stability (Green)

----------------------------------------
SECONDARY INDICATOR
----------------------------------------

Generate “Execution Stability” indicator derived ONLY from:

- Revenue ramp realism
- Revenue volatility
- Revenue type (recurring vs one-time)

This indicator does not affect total survival score.

----------------------------------------
PDF REPORT STRUCTURE
----------------------------------------

Generate structured PDF containing:

1. Cover Page
2. Executive Snapshot (Readiness Index + Interpretation)
3. Naive vs Adjusted Runway Comparison
4. True Independence Burn Breakdown
5. Liquidity-Weighted Net Worth Explanation
6. Revenue Assumptions
7. Base Case Scenario Page
8. -15% Scenario Page
9. +15% Scenario Page
10. Timeline Risk Curve (Month-by-month visualization)
11. Axis Breakdown Bars
12. Score Impact Levers (show how changes affect score)
13. Methodology Overview
14. Disclaimer Page

Language Rules:

- Calm
- Conservative
- Structured
- No motivational tone
- No advice language
- Always use phrasing:
  “Based on your selected assumptions…”

Include disclaimer:

"This report is an educational financial simulation based on user inputs and estimated U.S. averages. It is not financial, tax, or legal advice."

----------------------------------------
TECH REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------

- Mobile-first UI
- One-question-at-a-time input flow
- Deterministic math engine
- No AI-generated narrative required
- Server-side PDF generation
- Minimal data storage
- No banking integrations
- No live healthcare APIs

Build clean.
Build structured.
Do not expand scope.
Do not add features beyond this specification.
