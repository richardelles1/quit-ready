CORE CALCULATION ENGINE

Implement deterministic formulas:

⸻

1️⃣ True Monthly Independence Burn (TMIB)

TMIB =
Living Expenses
	•	Healthcare Estimate (based on selected type)
	•	Monthly Debt Payments
	•	Estimated Self-Employment Tax (calculate from revenue assumption, 25–30% conservative)
	•	Business Cost Baseline
− Partner Income

This is the primary structural metric.

⸻

2️⃣ Liquidity Haircuts

Apply conservative haircuts:

Cash: 100%
Brokerage: 80%
Roth contributions: 100%
Retirement accounts: 50%
Real estate equity: 30%

Accessible Capital =
Sum of (asset × haircut)

⸻

3️⃣ Runway Calculation

Runway Months =
Accessible Capital ÷ TMIB

If revenue is expected during ramp:
Discount revenue by volatility %
During ramp months, apply 50% realization factor.

Recalculate runway under:
	•	Base case
	•	-15% revenue
	•	-30% revenue
	•	Ramp delay

⸻

4️⃣ Debt Exposure Ratio

Debt Exposure =
Total Debt ÷ Accessible Capital

Flag:

If debt > 70% of accessible capital → Elevated structural risk

⸻

5️⃣ Structural Breakpoint Score (0–100)

Composite weighted score:

40% Runway strength
25% Revenue fragility
20% Debt exposure
10% Healthcare risk
5% Business cost density

Use nonlinear scoring curves:
	•	<6 months runway → severe penalty
	•	6–12 → moderate
	•	12–24 → stable
	•	24+ → strong
